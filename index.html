Common Issues & Solutions

Notifications Not Working

  1. Check Settings → Sound & Alerts → Notifications is enabled
  2. Go to iOS Settings → FlowState Timer → Notifications and ensure they are allowed
  3. Check that "Do Not Disturb" is not enabled on your device

Timer Not Accurate

  • Ensure the app is not forcekilled while timer is running
  • Check that Low Power Mode isn't affecting background tasks
  • Keep the app installed and don't delete while timer is running

Data Not Saving

  • Check that you have available storage on your device
  • Try force-quitting and reopening the app
  • If issues persist, try Settings → Data Management → Reset to Defaults

App Crashing

  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If problem persists, contact support with:
    • Your iOS version
    • Device model
    • Steps to reproduce the issue
